# DRIP Economy
- Yield: (Value * 0.0001) * (Hype / 100) per sec.
- Takeover: Price +20% each buy.
- Decay: 1% hype drop per minute.

# AI Algorithm (TikTok Style)
- **Feed Priority:** Assets are ranked by a dynamic score.
- **Viral Trigger:** If an asset is taken over 3 times in 10 mins, it gets a "VIRAL" tag and 2x Yield.
- **Discovery Mode:** 20% of the feed shows new assets (under 5 mins old) to ensure freshness.
- **AI Recommendation:** Future implementation will use Vector Similarity to show users assets matching their purchase history.
